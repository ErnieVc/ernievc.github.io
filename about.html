About

I spent my early years in a small city of Trujillo in Peru. After graduating secondary school (high-school), I moved to Utah to learn English. After completing BYU'S English as a Second Language Program (ESL), I enrolled in Utah Valley University. I was one of the first UVU Students to obtain a Bioinformatics degree. I consider myself a multifaceted person with interests in Pedagogy, Evolution, Phylogenetics, Bioinformatics.

Bioinformatician & Biologist

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Bioinformatics, an Associate's Degree in Biology, and a Minor in Digital Media.

  • Bachelor's Degree: Bioinformatics (UVU)
  • Associate Degree: Biology (UVU)
  • Minor: Digital Media (UVU)
  • Languages: English, Spanish
  • Email: ernie_vc@hotmail.com
  • Website: https://ernievc.github.io/

At UVU, I was not only able to receive an excellent education in a welcoming environment, but I also sought extra curricular activities that shaped my interpersonal skills. I was able to participate in undergraduate research projects and present at the Utah Conference for Undergraduate Research (UCUR) for 2020 and 2022 as well as the Entomological Society of America Conference for 2021 and 2022.

Also, I served as President of the International Student Council, Vice President and founding member of the 'Evolution and Bioinformatics Club.' My service in these positions allowed me to earn multiple scholarships and be awarded 2023's 'Biology Student Excelence Award for Leadership and Service.'
